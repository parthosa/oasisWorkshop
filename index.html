NSS Oasis Stall

NSS Oasis Stall is a kiosk to promote the relentless work of NGOs which strive to work towards social welfare.

The stall showcases numerous handicrafts and other products made by rural women and specially-abled, procured by NGOs. These include notebooks, diaries, candles, hand bags, t-shirts, and laptop bags among others.

NSS, BITS Pilani seeks to foster and honour the spirit and hard work of these NGOs who inspire and propel us to do our bit for the society, by spreading awareness about their work, through the stall.

All BITSians, participants and guests are welcome to visit the stall throughout the Oasis and take back few artefacts.

Timin gs: On all 4 days of OASIS from 9 A.M. to 11:30 P.M.

Venue: VK - Lawns
