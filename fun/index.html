ODYSSEY

Odyssey is a 48 hours Online puzzle solving event featuring many exciting puzzles based on the theme 'Around The World In 96 hours'. Puzzles will be of different types like pic - connect , pattern solving and mystery solving. So brace yourself for the most interesting quiz ever and test your knowledge about the World.

AGE OF ZOMBIES

Age Of Zombies is a multi-player gaming event where people fight to conquer territories. Four people who survive till the end of the world will have to capture all the territories to be the last man standing. Each player's territory will be guarded by zombies. Other players have to kill the zombies and capture the territory, at which point their zombies will spawn at that point. Come in Teams of Four!!

CITY SURFER

Ever Dreamt of Being inside a Game? Live the Dream this Oasis by being a part of City Surfer , a Motion Sensing live event where your movements on ground will help you surf the city by avoiding the obstacles on the screen . Run , Jump and Dodge your way to the prize.
